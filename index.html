About Me


My name is Christian Case, I've had a love for lifting weights and fitness for nearly as long as I can remember. It started as a New Years resolution for me when I was 10 years old to workout six times a week and not miss a day. During that time and every year since, I've discovered an intense passion for doing hard things for the sake of proving to myself that I can! Over the years fitness has become a part of who I am, I absolutely love to lift weights and that is what fuels every single workout for me. I truly believe that with proper nutrition, workout structure, and mindset that the sky is the limit for any and everyone's fitness. All it takes is starting, with no intention of stopping!

How Work For Your Masterpiece Works


Pick Your Program


First things first, you will pick between SHRED and BULK.

Depending on which program you pick you will be given a slightly different experience. Different workouts but the biggest difference will be the diets that are recommended.


Upload Beginning Photo


The next step in Work For Your Masterpiece is submitting your starting photo.

At the end of each month you will upload a different photo.

This will allow you to compare photos side by side each month to track the progress you are making.

This is also a way to help ensure you are consistently putting in the work it will take to see changes.


Starting Zoom Call


The next step will be a zoom call with Christian.

This will be to get some basic information so that I can build you your personalized workout program.


Begin Your Workouts


You will then be sent to the home page with modules of step by step workout for each day of the week.

There be recommended rep ranges and goals for you accomplish as well depending on you size and familiarity with fitness

There will also be modules of at home workouts that you can do if you do not have access to a gym.

Each week there will also be bonus challenges to test your comittment and to add a bit of fun.


Weekly Zoom Calls


At the end of each week you will have a 30 minute zoom call with Christian to go over any concerns or just recieve advice.

This will also help with your consistency since you will be held accountable during these calls.
